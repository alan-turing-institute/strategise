The game begins with each player putting $1 into the pot. A card is then dealt at random to Alice, who observes her card, while Bob does not. Alice must choose either to bet or to fold. If she folds, Bob wins the pot and the game ends. If she bets, she adds another $1 to the pot, after which Bob chooses whether to call or to fold. If Bob folds, Alice wins the pot and the game ends. If he calls, he adds another $1 to the pot and the game proceeds to a showdown, where Alice reveals her card. If her card is a King, she wins the pot; if her card is a Queen, Bob wins the pot.
